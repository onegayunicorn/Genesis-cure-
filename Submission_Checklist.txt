# SUBMISSION CHECKLIST (ODD APPLICATION)

## Pre-Submission
- [ ] LOI signed by Chief Regulatory Officer.
- [ ] ODD application package finalized (GC-DOC-002 version 1.3.0).
- [ ] FDA/TGA eCTD/eBS portal accounts verified and active.
- [ ] Regulatory contact list confirmed (Name, Title, Email, Phone).

## Submission
- [ ] Electronic submission via FDA ESG or TGA eBS portal.
- [ ] Certified mail copies sent (if required by local jurisdiction).
- [ ] Confirmation email sent to ODD@fda.hhs.gov and orphan.drugs@tga.gov.au.

## Post-Submission
- [ ] Track ODD/IND tracking number assignment.
- [ ] Monitor email communication for acknowledgment of receipt.
- [ ] Schedule Type B Pre-Submission Meeting (Target: July 15–August 15, 2026).
- [ ] Finalize pre-meeting package deliverables (IB, Safety Whitepaper, Validation Summaries) by July 15, 2026.
